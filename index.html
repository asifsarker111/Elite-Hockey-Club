Clients Question

Got questions? We’ve got answers! Here are some of the most frequently asked questions to help guide you.

What equipment do you have?

We offer premium hockey sticks, protective gear, and training tools to boost player performance and safety

What hockey training programs do you offer?

We offer a variety of hockey training programs, including skills development, team training sessions, and conditioning camps tailored for all skill levels.

Do you offer private hockey lessons?

Yes, we provide personalized private lessons tailored to individual skill levels, focusing on technique, strategy, and overall game improvement.

What is hockey touring?

Hockey touring refers to organized trips where players travel to participate in tournaments or training camps, enhancing their skills while experiencing new environments.

How do I book a hockey training session?

You can book a hockey training session through our website's booking page or by emailing us. We offer flexible schedules to suit your needs.

What are the pricing details for your programs?

Our programs vary in price depending on type and duration. For more information, contact us directly for a quote.
